To create a build from setup.py
py -m build (win32)
or
python3 -m build (unix)

to upload to pypi test repo
py -m twine upload --repository testpypi dist/* (win32)
or
python3 -m twine upload --repository testpypi dist/* --verbose (unix)

top upload to pypi official
py -m twine upload dist/* --verbose (win32)
or
python3 -m twine upload dist/* --verbose (unix)
